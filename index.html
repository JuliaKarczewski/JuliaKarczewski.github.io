Bashar Barakah Jackson

Bashar went by the name "Pop Smoke" and was a very successful American rapper.

July 20, 1999: Pop Smoke was born and raised in Brooklyn, New York in the small town Canarsie.

August, 2018: Late 2018, Pop Smoke's music career was beginning to start when he was invited to a studio by Jay Gwuapo.

January 28, 2019: Even though he recorded his first song "MPR", his first actual release was "Flexin'", which was realeased on January 28, 2019.

April, 2019: Pop officially signed to Victor Victor Worldwide, which was a subsidiary of Universal Music.

April 23, 2019: Pop released his breakout single "Welcome to the Party", which was later remixed by two famous celebrities that included Nicki Manaj and Skepta.

July 26, 2019: He released his debut mixtape, "Meet The Woo".

December 27, 2019: Pop Smoke appeared on Travis Scott's compilation album "JACKBOYS" on the song "GATTI" which was also accompanied with a music video.

January 16, 2020: Pop smoke released another single that would later be featured on his mixtape "Meet The Woo 2" called "Christopher Walking".

February 7, 2020: His second mixtape was released called, "Meet The Woo 2".

February 19, 2020: Pop Smoke passed away due to a fatal home invasion robbery located in Los Angeles, California.

July 3, 2020: Even though he passed, Pop was working on a new album, which was released after his death called "Shoot for the Stars, Aim for the Moon", which ended up being his most successful album. He still has hundreds of unreleased songs, which hopefully get realeased soon.
